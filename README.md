# 🌱 EcoDharma

## AI-Powered Smart Waste Management Platform

EcoDharma is an innovative waste management platform developed to address the growing challenge of improper waste disposal and environmental pollution. The platform empowers households and communities to classify waste correctly, discover recycling opportunities, participate in eco-friendly challenges, and earn rewards for sustainable actions.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

India generates approximately 1.7 lakh tonnes of municipal solid waste every day. A significant portion of this waste is either dumped in landfills or remains unaccounted for due to inefficiencies in the waste management chain.

Improper disposal methods such as open dumping, burning, and disposal into water bodies contribute significantly to:

* Environmental pollution
* Public health issues
* Land degradation
* Water contamination
* Greenhouse gas emissions

There is a strong need for a technology-driven solution that enables citizens to actively participate in waste management and recycling practices.

---

## 🎯 Our Solution

EcoDharma provides a complete ecosystem for sustainable waste management through:

### ♻️ AI Waste Classification

Users can upload an image of waste material and the AI model automatically identifies the category of waste.

Categories include:

* Plastic Waste
* Organic Waste
* Paper Waste
* Glass Waste
* Metal Waste
* Electronic Waste

### 💡 Personalized Reuse & DIY Suggestions

The platform generates creative ideas for reusing waste materials.

Examples:

* Plastic Bottle Planters
* DIY Storage Containers
* Decorative Crafts
* Composting Solutions

### 🏆 Reward System

Users earn Eco Points for:

* Recycling activities
* Waste segregation
* Participating in eco challenges
* Completing sustainability tasks

These points encourage consistent environmental responsibility.

### 📍 Recycler Locator

Using Google Maps integration, users can:

* Find nearby recycling centers
* Locate collection points
* Discover waste disposal facilities
* Navigate directly to selected locations

### 🎮 Leaderboard & Challenges

Gamification features include:

* Weekly Challenges
* Monthly Competitions
* Community Leaderboards
* Sustainability Missions

This creates healthy competition and increases community participation.

### 🏠 Household-Centric Design

The platform is designed specifically for:

* Families
* Residential Communities
* Apartment Complexes
* Educational Institutions
* Organizations
